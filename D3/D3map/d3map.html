Percentage of forest area/land area per country

I choose these yellow to green colors for forestation of the land, because it makes intuitive sense that more forest is greener, and less forrest is less green. Other colors would make it more complicated than it needs to be. Using only two colors decreases the chance that you need to legend to understand the color-coding of the map. People like their forests to be green.
